# Key Performance Indicator (KPI) Definitions and Formulas

This document serves as the single source of truth for financial and operational metric definitions across all analytics dashboards.

## 1. Revenue (Net Sales)
- **Definition**: The total amount of money generated by sales of products or services after subtracting returns, allowances, and promotional discounts.
- **Formula**:
  `Revenue = Sum of (Quantity * Unit Price) - Total Discounts`

## 2. Profit (Net Profit)
- **Definition**: The financial gain remaining after subtracting all operational costs, cost of goods sold (COGS), discounts, and tax expenses from net revenue.
- **Formula**:
  `Profit = Revenue - Total Cost`

## 3. Profit Margin (Gross/Net Margin)
- **Definition**: A metric representing the profitability of a product, segment, or region, expressed as a percentage of total revenue.
- **Formula**:
  `Profit Margin (%) = (Profit / Revenue) * 100`

## 4. Growth Rate (Revenue Growth)
- **Definition**: The percentage increase or decrease in net revenue over a specific period (e.g., month-over-month or year-over-year).
- **Formula**:
  `Growth Rate (%) = ((Revenue_Current_Period - Revenue_Prior_Period) / Revenue_Prior_Period) * 100`

## 5. Conversion Rate
- **Definition**: The percentage of website visitors or sales prospects who complete a desired action (e.g., make a purchase).
- **Formula**:
  `Conversion Rate (%) = (Number of Conversions / Total Number of Visitors) * 100`

## 6. Average Order Value (AOV)
- **Definition**: The average amount of money spent by a customer per transaction.
- **Formula**:
  `Average Order Value = Total Revenue / Total Number of Orders`

## 7. Churn Rate (Customer Churn)
- **Definition**: The percentage of customers who stop subscribing to or buying from the business during a given time frame.
- **Formula**:
  `Churn Rate (%) = (Customers Lost during Period / Customers at Start of Period) * 100`
